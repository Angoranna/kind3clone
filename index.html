for your body

We believe if you can't pronounce an
ingredient, it shouldn’t go into your body.
Actually, it shouldn’t even go into your pantry.
KIND® products are made from
nutritionally-dense ingredients like whole
nuts, fruits and whole grains - no secret
ingredients and no artificial flavors,
preservatives or sweeteners.

about Daniel

Daniel Lubetzky, founder and CEO of KIND, is on a mission to
make the world a little kinder one snack and act at a time. A
pioneering social entrepreneur working to build bridges
between people through innovative models, Daniel is also the
founder of PeaceWorks Inc., a business that fosters cooperative
ventures among neighbors in the Middle East.
